Check-in

Arrival — self check-in in 2 steps

1

Key to the main complex entrance

From the pavement below the apartment balcony you'll see a small white box with a lizard on it. Call us — on our cue we'll open it remotely and a key box will drop out. Please return it once you're inside.

2

Apartment No. 522 A

There's a code lock on the door frame — we'll send you the code before arrival. Inside you'll find all the keys (security door, upper and lower locks, complex entrance / pool / bins). A second set of keys is in the apartment for your convenience.

02

Complex & pool

Entrances
The complex has 4 entrances — the main one, 2 side and one upper. A single universal key works for all of them, for the pool gate, the pool toilets and for the general-waste bins across the street.
Waste
General waste: across the street (green gate in front of Malibu Park Hotel). Recycling: right opposite. Please don't leave food on the terrace — it attracts insects.
Drinking water
Filtered water is available on the ground floor for €1 / 5 l. Please use only filtered water in appliances (coffee machine, kettle).
Pool
On the rooftop. Sun loungers, parasols and guest toilets are provided. You can reach it by stairs, by lift (+ a few steps), or via the step-free side entrance.
Quiet hours
Please keep it quiet from 11 pm to 9 am — our complex is family-friendly and peaceful. Parties are not allowed. Thank you for respecting other guests and neighbours. 🙏

04

Getting around Tenerife

Taxi / car
Taxis and car-rental desks are available right at the airport. There's plenty of free on-street parking near the apartment. Please do not park on the pavement — your vehicle may be towed and you risk a fine.
Bus
The Av. de los Pueblos stop is a 15-min walk away. TITSA buses reliably cover the whole island, including the airport and the capital. Large luggage goes in the hold for free. Handy app: Google Maps.
Bikes, scooters, mopeds
There are several rental shops for bikes, mopeds and electric vehicles nearby. A shared e-scooter station is located right in front of the complex.

Inside the apartment

Kitchen

You have a fully equipped kitchen: stove, oven, microwave, coffee machine, kettle, toaster, grill, juicer, portable induction hob, paella pan and a full set of cookware.

Switch the kitchen under-counter lighting on with the slider on the extractor hood.

Ceiling fan
Turn it on with the wall switch, then confirm with the remote. The remote's button also controls the light.
Smart TV
Sign in with your own streaming services; the Skylink app is also available.
Washing machine
You'll find the washing machine in the bathroom. Laundry detergent is ready to go — feel free to use it. 👕
Terrace

The plants are watered automatically. Sun loungers are in the wardrobe.

Please always close the parasol when you leave the apartment — the wind can pick up unexpectedly and could knock it over.

The terrace security camera is only active between guest stays — during your stay it is switched off automatically, and you can additionally cover it with the shutter.
